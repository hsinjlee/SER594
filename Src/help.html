About our website

We are the courseexchange Patnala team, we are responsible for developing an application which will allow interaction between students, faculty and advisor in a nice and elegant way. So, we are suggesting that we need a website for - Knowing the demand for the course, Getting to know the professor, Waitlist for the courses, A communication gateway for the students to exchange courses and negotiate with, A notification generator if there is any unexpected vacancy in the desired course, and compare two courses to decide which one better suit you. We will have a chance to design a profile for each student to show what courses she/he is planning to take or has already enrolled, For each Professor to show the courses he is teaching and maybe some information about his previous courses, A chat box linked with asu email, A page to show the comparison between any two courses, A pop-up notification/ email to notify vacancies, and Automation registers from IPOS.
