About our website

What you can do !!

This is website where you can:
1. Know the demand for the course.
2. Get to know the professor.
3. Waitlist for the courses.
4. A communication gateway for the students to exchange courses and negotiate with.
5. A notification generator if there is any unexpected vacancy in the desired course.
6. Compare two courses to decide which one better suit you.
7. We will have a chance to design a profile for each student to show what courses she/he is planning to take or has already enrolled.
8. For each Professor to show the courses he is teaching and maybe some information about his previous courses.
9. A chat box linked with asu email.
10. A page to show the comparison between any two courses.
11. A pop-up notification/ email to notify vacancies. Automation registers from IPOS.
12. Add or withdraw a course
